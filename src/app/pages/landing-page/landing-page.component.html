Fallout Forge

Build, Survive, Thrive

Step into the shoes of a post-apocalyptic overseer, tasked with the monumental responsibility of managing three settlements in a harsh and unforgiving world. In this immersive game experience inspired by the Fallout universe, you'll embark on a journey of survival, resource management, and strategic decision-making.

Manage Three Unique Settlements

Each settlement is a bastion of hope in a desolate landscape, but they're far from self-sufficient. You'll need to carefully allocate resources, oversee construction projects, and ensure the well-being of your settlers. From the bustling markets of Jamaica Plains to the rugged outposts of Abernathy Farm, every settlement presents its own challenges and opportunities.

Craft, Build, and Expand

Utilize the remnants of a bygone era to craft essential structures and utilities that will aid in the survival and growth of your settlements. Build farms to feed your populace, construct defenses to fend off threats, and erect trading posts to establish vital connections with other survivors. As you level up, unlock new technologies and capabilities to fortify your settlements and secure their future.

Trade and Barter

In a world where resources are scarce, mastering the art of trade is essential for survival. Engage in commerce with traders, strike lucrative deals, and stockpile valuable goods to bolster your settlements' prosperity.

Strategic Shipping

Connect your settlements through trade routes and supply lines, enabling the efficient exchange of resources and goods. Whether it's transporting essential supplies or reinforcing defenses, strategic shipping is the lifeline that binds your settlements together and ensures their resilience against the myriad dangers of the wasteland..

Level Up to Prosper

As your settlements grow and prosper, so too will your influence as an overseer. Increase your level to expand your settlement capacity, allowing for greater resource storage, more advanced constructions, and enhanced defenses. Only by continually improving the safety and quality of life in your settlements can you hope to thrive in this harsh new world.

Are You Ready to Lead?

The fate of humanity rests in your hands. Will you rise to the challenge and guide your settlements to a brighter tomorrow? Prepare for a journey of grit, determination, and survival unlike any other in Fallout Forge. The wasteland awaits your leadership.
